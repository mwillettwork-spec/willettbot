Record a new script
Pick your hotkeys, click Start, then do the thing you want to automate — clicks, typing, and keystrokes are captured in real time. Press your end hotkey to finish, then review and save.
First time recording? macOS will ask for permission to watch your keystrokes and to control Finder / System Events / any apps you use — click OK on every prompt. If you miss one, you can re-enable it in System Settings → Privacy & Security → Automation. Without Automation permission the recorder only captures raw clicks, not app switches or file opens.
Hotkeys are optional — the Start/Stop buttons below always work. Use Esc or Return if Fn keys don't fire on your Mac.
